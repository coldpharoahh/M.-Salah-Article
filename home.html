About Mohammed Salah

Mohammed Salah is one of the most well-known football players in the world. He is renowned for his resilience, speed, and great footballing qualities. He is a great person on, and off the pitch. Let's take a look at his incredible achievements!

Achievements And Statistics

  1. His hunger for goals - Salah scored 32 goals in 2018 to break the English Premier League goal record, and he was the first African player to do it!
  2. His personal trophy cabinet which includes:
    1. 1x Champions League
    2. 2x African Player of the Year Awards
    3. 2x Footballer of the Year Awards
    4. 1x Club World Cup
    5. 1x English Premier League

And there are of course, many more trophies to come!
